🎮 Snake, Water, Gun Game

This is a 10-round console-based game where the player competes against the computer.

The rules follow classic logic:

  • 🐍 Snake drinks water
  • 💧 Water defeats gun
  • 🔫 Gun kills snake

After 10 rounds, a winner is announced based on the scores.

🔧 Open your browser console (Press F12)
Type or call: StartGame() to begin playing.
